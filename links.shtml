Bi 188 Spring 2018 - Useful Links


  • Online Mendelian Inheritance in Man (OMIM) - a catalog of human genes and genetic disorders authored and edited by Dr. Victor A. McKusick and his colleagues at Johns Hopkins and elsewhere, and developed for the World Wide Web by NCBI, the National Center for Biotechnology Information
  • RepeatMasker - a good program for masking out repeat sequence
  • GeneCards - a database of human genes, their products and their involvement in diseases. It offers concise information about the functions of all human genes that have received official names
  • MUSSA - an n-way sequence comparison program
  • UCSC Genome Browser - a database and browser of various genomes, including mouse and human
  • GNF SymAtlas - an expression database run by the Genomics Institute of the Novartis Research Foundation
  • Gene Ontology - a database of Gene Ontology (GO) numbers for various genes
  • The International HapMap Project
  • 1000 Genomes Project - The 1000 Genomes Project
  • dbSNP - a database of SNPs for various organisms
  • ENCODE Project - website of the ENCODE project
  • ENCODE Project at UCSC - the ENCODE project at UCSC
  • modENCODE - the modENCODE project (fly and worm)
  • SAM format specifications - SAM format specifications
