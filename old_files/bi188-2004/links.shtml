Useful Links


  • Online Mendelian Inheritance in Man - a catalog of human genes and genetic disorders authored and edited by Dr. Victor A. McKusick and his colleagues at Johns Hopkins and elsewhere, and developed for the World Wide Web by NCBI, the National Center for Biotechnology Information
  • RepeatMasker - a good program for masking out repeat sequence
  • GeneCards - a database of human genes, their products and their involvement in diseases. It offers concise information about the functions of all human genes that have received official names
