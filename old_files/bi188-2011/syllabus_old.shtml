    Authors: J. Watson, A. Caudy, R. Myers, and J. Witkowski
    ISBN: 0-7167-2866-4

    General Notes:

    1. As the book and lectures will not be highly redundant, the book is intended as background material for which you will be responsible. It is suggested that after the first week you complete the reading before coming to the lecture. Chapters 1-7 are for filling in and brushing up on relevant molecular biology. For most of you, this will be a light review activity.
    2. Most lectures will include additional reading from the literature. Generally this will include one or two review or summary pieces (which are best to read first) and several pertinent deeper research articles. These will accompany the lecture (distributed by e-mail). With the exception of preprints, most papers will be from journals accessible on-line.
    3. There will be a midterm, a genome analysis project paper, and a final exam.
